Component Select:
It’s not KBC, nor is it a CAT exam, it’s BIGGER and BETTER. It’s a platform for you to prove, that you are a true IT ‘an. 3 racy and breathtaking rounds of quizzing and the one to emerge victorious is definitely the KING of IT.
Description:
FIRST ROUND:
Initially, a page which consists of twenty to twenty five folders with component names (i.e.., Computer, RAM etc..,) hidden in it is shown. The participants are asked to choose two folders randomly. Depending upon their choice of the folder the component will be displayed and the questions related to that component will be asked .The participants will score their points for the correct answer. Top six contestants will be forwarded to the second round.
SECOND ROUND:
This round is to test their language skills. A page with five or six buttons with language names will be displayed. The participant can choose any one of the folder according to his priority of knowledge he has for the particular language. Question will be asked and points will be scored by participants. Negative marking will be there in this round.

   THIRD ROUND:
At this level the price of the components will be fixed and revealed to participants. It is a buzzer round. If he answers the question correctly then he will be asked to buy any component. In this way the person who buys more components and frames one or more PCs will be the winner.
