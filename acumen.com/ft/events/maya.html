Hands on workshop on Animation:
Acumen IT 2011 beckons all those students with an artistic flair and those aspiring Steven Speilbergs, James Camerons and cartoon lovers to take part in the extremely interactive and entertaining session that will be held by MAAC (Maya Academy of Animation) on the 4th and 5th of march 2011. The participants will be shown first hand how mind boggling visual effects for movies like 300, Avatar, Kungfu Panda were created. By the end of the session, the participants will be able to create their own stunning visual effects and impress their peers. So come and attend this session to impress your lecturers , boy friends and gal friends.!!!
