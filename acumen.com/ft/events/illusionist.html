The Illusionist:
What you see is not always what it really is. It takes a magician to break the code of an illusion. Are you the magician we are looking for? If yes, then prove it to the world at The Illusionist event at Acumet IT 2011.
Description:
This event requires a team of 2 to participate. There are 3 levels. In the first level the team that clears the cutoff making use of the clues can advance to the next level. In the second and third levels the questions will get tougher but the teams can take the help of the lifelines to reach THE ILLUSIONIST and claim their reward.
