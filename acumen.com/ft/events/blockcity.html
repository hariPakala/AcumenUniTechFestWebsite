Block City:
RULES:
1. The number of players in the game should be 4.
2. When the game starts, the 4 players are the center of each of the sides of the grid and each player is given 3 fences.
3. The player who hits the buzzer first will be given a chance to answer the question, which has to be done within the specified time of 30 seconds.
4. If the player answers correctly within the time he/she gets the opportunity to choose to move forward or to put up one of his fences. When he/she has run out of fences, the player must move forward.
    ->Players moves:
         The player can move forward one square at a time, horizontally or vertically, forwards or backwards.
    ->Positioning of the fences
         The fences can be placed anywhere. The fences can be used to facilitate the player’s progress or to impede that of the opponent; however, an access to the goal line must always be left open. 
  5. If the player fails to answer correctly within the time his/her away would be blocked as per the wish of the host.    
  6. Face to face:
   When two players face each other on neighboring squares which are not separated by a fence, the player whose turn it is can jump  the opponent(and place himself behind him), thus advancing an extra square ). If there is a fence behind the said player, the player can move to the left or the right of the other player.
