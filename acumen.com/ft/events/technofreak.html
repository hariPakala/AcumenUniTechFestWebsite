Techno Freak:
If you think that you are the most technically sound person in this city, If you think you are the GOD of IT, Well…think again!!!  It’s not about showing off your divinity but, it’s all about freaking people out of their wits with your knowledge. Here is your chance to do exactly that…..Attend this event at Acumen IT and prove it to the world that you indeed are a FREAK-A TECHO FREAK.
General rules:
The quiz consists of 3 rounds.

  1. Each round has 2 sections.
  2. 2 teams with lowest scores will be eliminated at the end of each round.

Round I

    • An’tech’shari

àEvery team should name a word that begins with the last alphabet of the word said by the preceding team.
àThe words should strictly be within the domain on which the quiz is based on.

    • # Define

          àA definition will be read out and the team has to name the concept or term.
Round II

    1. Coding Fundas

          àBasic questions related to coding fundamentals will be asked.

    1. True/False

          à A code and its output are shown the team has to answer if the output is correct or wrong.

Round III

    1. Rapid Fire

          à Questions will be fired as long as the time expires.

    1. Guess the word

          à A definition of a concept is read out; the team has to guess the concept. Clues can be taken but at the cost of some points.
Co –ordinators           : Nagaraju, K. Nithya.
Organizers                : Pooja, Anudeep, Jagdeesh, Vinay.
